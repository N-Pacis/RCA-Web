Admission policies



To consider the master our level of education provided by our school for each student. We select students who scored higher grades in Mathematics, Physics and English each year in the o'level national exams.

The following are criteria used to admit students to our school:


  1. Having scored highest grades in Mathematics,Physics and English and in the top 60 students in the mentioned subjects.
  2. Having chosen Rwanda coding academy on the registration form.
  3. Having the best total grades in O'level national exams.

Students who study at Rwanda Coding Academy are selected by Rwanda Education Board after the release of marks of the o'level national exams.

I should choose Rwanda Coding Academy If:

  • I'm interested to acquire lessons including Embedded systems,Cyber security and Software engineering.
  • I'm ready to work hard to achieve what the country expects from me.
  • I have great passion to study software engineering at an early age.
  • I'm interested in solving IT problems in the future.
